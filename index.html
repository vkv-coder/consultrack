ConsulTrack — Privacy Policy
Our Promise to You
✅ We NEVER sell your data
✅ We NEVER share your data with anyone
✅ We NEVER show ads using your data
✅ You can delete your data anytime
Data Storage

Your professional fee data is stored with bank-level AES-256 encryption on Supabase servers located in Mumbai, India. Your data never leaves India.

Only you can access your data through your personal login. VKV Coder staff do not access, view or process your personal fee information.

What We Store (On YOUR Behalf)

• Your name and mobile — for access only
• Fee data YOU enter — stored securely for YOUR access only
• Your self-set PIN — stored as a hash, we cannot read it

We do NOT collect, read or process your personal fee data. Your data is stored FOR YOU, not BY US.

We do NOT collect your location, contacts, photos or any device information.

🔒 Think of us as a bank locker —
We hold it safely, but only YOU have the key!
Your Rights

• Access: All your data is visible in the app
• Export: Download your data as Excel/PDF anytime
• Delete: Request complete deletion anytime
• Portability: Your data belongs to you
