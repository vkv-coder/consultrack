ConsulTrack — Privacy Policy
✅ We NEVER sell your data
✅ We NEVER share your data with anyone
✅ We NEVER show ads using your data
✅ You can delete your data anytime

Data Storage

Your professional fee data is stored with bank-level AES-256 encryption on Supabase servers in Mumbai, India. Your data never leaves India.

Only you can access your data. VKV Coder staff cannot view or process your fee records.

What We Store (On YOUR Behalf)

• Your name and mobile — for access only
• Fee data YOU enter — stored for YOUR access only
• Your PIN — stored as a hash, we cannot read it

We do NOT collect your location, contacts, photos or device info.

🔒 Think of us as a bank locker —
We hold it safely, but only YOU have the key!

Your Rights

• Access: All data visible in app
• Export: Download as Excel/PDF anytime
• Delete: Request deletion anytime

Security
